% Priors for the chabrier_smooth_bounds IMF fit.
% All priors are uniform within the stated bounds (implemented as a smooth
% quadratic barrier for NUTS gradient safety; see salpyter/sampling.py).
% \sigma is the lognormal width in dex; the sampled coordinate is \ln\sigma.
All priors are uniform:
$\log m_c \in [-2, 2]$,
$\ln\sigma \in [-2, 2]$,
$\Gamma \in [-10, 2]$,
$\log m_{\min} \in [-4, 4]$,
$\log m_{\max} \in [-4, 4]$ (all masses in $M_\odot$).
